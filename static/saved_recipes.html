How To Use Our Saved Recipes

Once you've saved a recipe by clicking the "Save Recipe" star, your recipe will be added here. You can make changes to your recipes here, for example, if the recipe needed more salt than advised, you could make a note to add more salt next time! This page is also a hub for recipes you add. You can add to our growing repository of recipes and share your favorite dishes with others!
